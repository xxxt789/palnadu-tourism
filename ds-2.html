Ethipothala Falls

Ethipothala Falls, a stunning cascade of water that tumbles down from a height of approximately 70 feet, is located in the Gunturdistrict of Andhra Pradesh, India. This natural spectacle is formed by the confluence of three streams, namely Chandravanka Vagu, Nakkala Vagu and Tummala Vagu.
